🌸 New Bloom

Bloom into deeper self-discovery

New Bloom is an informational self-discovery website designed to help users understand themselves through multiple systems. Chinese Zodiac provides personality insight based on birth year. Numerology analyzes names and birth dates to calculate core numbers. Birth Chart introduces astrology through birth date, time, and place, with room to expand into full natal chart analysis later.

Welcome to New Bloom

Chinese Zodiac explains personality traits, strengths, challenges, and career tendencies based on birth year.

Numerology calculates Life Path, Expression, Soul Urge, Personality, and Personal Year to offer insight into life direction and inner motivations.

Birth Chart introduces astrology using birth details to identify core placements such as sun sign, with room for future backend API expansion.
